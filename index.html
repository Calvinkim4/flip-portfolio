Hacker-Man #1

Calvin Kim

The Origin

This is the story of an ambitious software engineer named Calvin Kim and how he started programming.
Before Calvin started to code, he was a night-time stocker at a Grocery Store.
During one of his usual night-time shifts, he was bitten by a spider...
After his encouraging words, the spider crawled away.
Deciding to take the spider's word to heart, Calvin went home and started studying Java instead of watching shows as usual.
He started building a mobile android game using an open-source framework called Libgdx. He called the game JumperFall.
He encountered many problems which caused sadness, anger, confusion, and despair but would always come out victorious and happy.
After finishing JumperFall, He built many more games and fought many enemies.
Calvin felt he needed to expand his knowledge so he learned web development.
With a variety of new skills. he created multiple web applications.
